How OrganLink Works

Simple, transparent, and life-saving — from registration to transplant coordination

1

Register Patient & Donor

Hospitals securely register patients and living/deceased donors with complete medical history, blood type, and urgency level.

2

Intelligent Matching

Our algorithm finds compatible donor-recipient pairs based on blood group, tissue typing, urgency score, and geographic proximity.

3

Transplant & Save Lives

Hospital approves the match, schedules transplant procedure, and gives a second chance at life — full audit trail maintained.
